Niacinamide FAQ

Is 10% niacinamide too strong?

Many people tolerate 10% well, but some sensitive skins prefer 2–5%. If you notice redness or stinging, step down in strength or use it less often.

Can I use niacinamide with vitamin C?

Modern dermatology consensus is that they can be used together. If your skin is sensitive, you can still separate them (vitamin C in the morning, niacinamide at night) for comfort.

How long does it take to see results?

Many people notice less oiliness and smoother texture within 2–4 weeks. More even tone and fewer marks can take 8–12 weeks or longer.
