Privacy & Cookies

How Niacinimide.com handles analytics data and affiliate links.

1. Who we are

Niacinimide.com is a product comparison website operated by ronarn AS, Norway. We review and compare over-the-counter niacinamide products.

2. Data we collect

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site. This includes:

  • Which pages are visited and how often
  • Approximate country and device type
  • Referring websites (where visitors came from)

IP addresses are processed by Google in an anonymised way. We do not try to identify individual users.

3. Cookies

Analytics and affiliate tracking may use cookies. When you click through to retailers such as Amazon, those sites will set their own cookies according to their privacy policies.

4. Affiliate links

Some outbound links on Niacinimide.com are affiliate links. If you buy through these links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This does not affect our review process or rankings.
