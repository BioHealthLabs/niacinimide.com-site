Best Niacinamide for Beginners

Niacinamide is one of the most versatile skincare ingredients. It can help with oil control, redness, post-acne marks and overall texture – but only if your skin actually tolerates it.

1. Start with the right strength

  • 2–5%: gentle starting point for sensitive or reactive skin.
  • 5–10%: sweet spot for most people – visible results without being extreme.
  • 15–20%: “booster” territory; best for experienced users.

2. How often should you use it?

Most beginners do well with once daily, or every other day if they are already using actives like retinoids or acids. Niacinamide is flexible – morning or evening both work.

3. Where does niacinamide go in the routine?

  1. Cleanser
  2. Optional toner / mist
  3. Niacinamide serum
  4. Moisturiser
  5. SPF 30+ in the morning
